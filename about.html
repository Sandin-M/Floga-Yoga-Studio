Yoga is for everyone

We are a Yoga studio located in Munich. We believe that Yoga is a practice for the body and the mind. It is also an ethical exercise, where the physical practice is an execution of the meditative aspect. We aim to bring balance into our lives using yogic practices in their traditional forms and different styles.

We believe in the power of hands-on assists. Kindly tell your teacher before the class starts if you wish not to be assisted.

Yoga is for everyone. All bodies are welcome.

The Founder

Behind Floga Yoga Studio is Florian Behling.Having personally experienced the significant positive impact of Yoga on the body, mind, and well-being, he seeks to create a welcoming space to share these benefits with a broader community. He believes in the power of life-long learning. Therefore, he aims to build a yogic environment where people are guided by experienced and professional teachers with inspiring stories and diverse experiences. For him, Floga embodies a space with a sense of community at its core, where the teachers are enablers of the journey and not at its center.
